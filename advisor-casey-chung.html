to Commercial Execution

Casey (Kichung) Chung is a business and technology strategist with more than 25 years of experience driving growth through product innovation, strategic partnerships, and market expansion across global technology industries. His background spans AI, cloud, machine learning, cybersecurity, semiconductors, and enterprise technology — giving him a rare ability to connect technical differentiation with commercial execution. He is particularly experienced in helping companies shape go-to-market strategy, build ecosystem partnerships, and navigate cross-border growth opportunities.

Mr. Chung previously served as Head of Business Development at AWS Korea, where he led national growth initiatives and advised C-level leaders on cloud transformation. He also held executive roles at SK Infosec and Oracle, and spent more than a decade at Samsung Electronics leading strategy, partnerships, and emerging technology initiatives. Earlier in his career, he worked at Samsung Advanced Institute of Technology in visual intelligence.

He holds an MBA from MIT Sloan School of Management.

At Valley Innovation Partners, Mr. Chung advises companies on AI and cloud strategy, business development, strategic alliances, and U.S. market expansion.
